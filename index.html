About me

I am a researcher at TCS Research and Innovation. I am part of the Embedded Systems & Robotics (ESR) group. My current research focus includes enabling human-like interaction between a robot and humans. I am currently working on natural language understanding for HRI, dialogue agents for robotic task execution and speech recognition in telepresence robotics. I briefly worked on energy-efficient crowd-sensing techniques employed on mobile phones.
